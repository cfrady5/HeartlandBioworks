About Heartland BioWorks

Heartland BioWorks is Indiana's federally designated biomanufacturing Regional Tech Hub, powered by the Applied Research Institute. The Hub connects workforce, innovation, manufacturing, and research assets to accelerate biotechnology growth across Indiana.

Our Mission

To grow Indiana's biomanufacturing economy by connecting the people, companies, research institutions, and government partners that make biotechnology possible — building the workforce, the commercialization support, and the resource network the industry needs to scale in Indiana.

What We Do

Heartland BioWorks coordinates Indiana's biomanufacturing ecosystem as one integrated effort. We build the skilled workforce employers need, help innovators commercialize biotechnology, and connect companies to the manufacturing, lab, and funding resources available across the state — anchored by a planned headquarters and hands-on training facility in Indianapolis.

Our Programs

Three core programs work together to move biotech solutions from idea to impact:

BioTrain

Workforce development through stackable credentials, employer upskilling and reskilling, and statewide training pathways — no four-year degree required.

BioLaunch

Commercialization and innovation support for biotechnology solutions, including BioStart peer-to-peer mentorship for founders.

BioCAN

The BioResource Coordination and Access Network connecting innovators with Indiana's CDMOs, labs, and scale-up resources.
